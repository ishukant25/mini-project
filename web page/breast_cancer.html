Breast Cancer Detection

What is Breast Cancer?

Breast cancer is a disease in which cells in the breast grow out of control. It is one of the most common types of cancer worldwide.

Early Detection & Prevention

  • Perform regular self-examinations.
  • Schedule routine mammograms.
  • Maintain a healthy lifestyle.
  • Know your family history.
  • Consult a doctor if you notice any unusual changes.
